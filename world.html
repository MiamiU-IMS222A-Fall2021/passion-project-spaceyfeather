The Realm

Vriodel is a vast expanse of forests and mountains home primarily to woodland fauna and the occasional mythical creature. It is often cloudy, obscuring the sun and keeping the level of light constant throughout the day. The night sky is always full of life, and the stars shine clearly no matter how bright and full the moon may be.

Dawn and Dusk Peaks

Two towering mountains in the center of the realm, shaped almost like a pair of crows. A thin stone bridge connects them. The surrounding mountains have been reduced to rubble, but many creatures still call them home. Dusk Peak is riddled with small caves, and it is rumored that Adaeth resides in one of them.

Ruins of Laybourn

A once great castle made by the first human settlers in Vriodel. It was destroyed by Vykan after his mate Akantha was slain by the knights in a foolish test of power.

Raines

A village that was connected to Laybourn but became a home solely for the mythical inhabitants of Vriodel after the destruction of the castle. A harsh wind blows down from Dusk Peak , often putting the villagers at risk if they are caught outside in one of the snowstorms.

Evairn

A quiet forest that experiences a constant drizzle of rain. The flora provides shelter, making it a nice place to be if one can stand a damp pelt. A network of rivers runs through it and creates a great waterfall flowing into Nari below.

Nari/Mari

Also known as the Light Forest, it is relatively young and spread out. The trees capture sunlight, providing a soft yellow light throughout the night and darker days.

Locke

Thought to be the oldest forest, it is a grove of thick pines towering over the rest of the realm on a high cliff, and rather devoid of life.

Waleryn

A dry moor that appears to be trapped in an eternal sunset. A mountain stands in the middle, housing an intricate cave system with a lake deep underground. The moor is known to have an excess of magic that can drive one mad.

Farrich

A peculiar dragon-shaped island often shrouded in mist. No one is quite sure of what or who is housed there, as it's too long a fly and too dangerous a swim.

Palicie Facility

A facility created by humans who have snuck their way into the realm, nestled on one of the cliffs below Locke. There they test on whatever creatures they can find, hoping for some kind of breakthrough. Their goal is unknown.

Tarri

A cheerful town built primarily along one wide path. They live peacefully, and give thanks to the guardians, especially Delta and Mirrin, who visit every equinox.

Ni'rr

An incredibly complex and dangerous forest full of lost souls and vengeful spirits. The deeper into the mire one travels, the more it distorts, trapping them forever.

The Out-Where

An expanse of golden fields created by Adaeth in an attempt to isolate the realm. Where the forest becomes hazy and a line of stones lays in front of your path, turn back.

Velz

Great dragons thought to be the original inhabitants of Vriodel, formed by the ancient dragon Vren. All Velz are given a name that has at least one letter of Vren's, to honor them. Their two tails harbor immense destructive and healing capabilities, housed in the bone-like blades and large tufts of fur respectively.
Few exist today, leading to numerous theories. Some believe that they passed their energy into the realm, absorbed by the earth, while others claim they changed form to be commoners, and the most daring suggest that they were all slain.

Guardians

Powerful creatures tasked to watch over the realm with their lives, distinguished by the markings most share - namely the stripes leading from their eyes to the end of their face. These stripes can become jagged and disconnected if a guardian is stripped of their status and power.
