Welcome to Vriodel

...or more accurately, welcome to a snapshot of it. With nothing but the most sarcastic, unhelpful of explanations.

What is this?

WtV is a fantasy story that's been in the works for... 4 years? Possibly? The earliest music dates back to 2015, characters to 2016, writings around 2017. There is, in fact, a LOT of writing, but I am tired and they are old and this project is taking more time than I thought (it's okay though this is the most enjoyable one).
The story primarily focuses on loss, changes, and grief. Primarily, it served as a coping mechanism, and I hope to be able to turn it into a reflection on mental health.

Where is this going?

We have no idea!
It's been a number of ideas over time. A webcomic, written work, 3D game (more open-world), who knows anymore. Hopefully I can get a better idea as I continue with college and expand my creative abilities.
There have been two (failed) discord servers with the intent to share and continue work on it. (Entirely my fault, as I've had zero social outlet or following of not-friends anywhere for a while now.) In fact, save for my professor, you're likely one of those people! I am so sorry that 2016 me existed. /lh
If you're interested in this story, have questions, or anything really, please reach out to me! I have a few contacts linked below (along with my ko-fi, where I take commissions).
