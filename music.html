Music

There are two established themes - the main and the waltz. However, what exists is mostly improv, and 99% A minor. The simplistic and repetitive motifs stem from 1. Me being stressed 2. Not having much time home alone (the only time I could comfortably play) and 3. Not absorbing much knowledge from piano lessons.
The first two recordings were (stupidly) done by putting my phone on the piano and taking a video. Honestly, the audio quality of all the clips are kinda bad. Not to mention that these all walk a fine line between "painful" and "enjoyable" due to fumbling around with no guidance.

Harmonia was recorded in September 2016 and features the main theme. My dog Murphy can be heard barking at the end.

Safe Return was recorded a year later in August 2017 and remains the longest recording. Thanks to the way I recorded, my singing is barely audible. I cannot remember the words.

Thoughts in D Minor was recorded October 2019 along with Mix of the Mind. They were not done with WtV in mind, but still fit the overall tone.

Mix of the Mind is a constant back-and-forth, meant to represent the difficulty of keeping balance with mental health.

The Choir was made with GarageBand in June 2019 for Ohio Northern University's summer music camp. I think I was the only one to not remix an already existing song. Unfortunately, the GarageBand file wasn't saved, so this clip remains a bit.. awkwardly timed.

Do You Know was recorded February 2019 on flute and features me complaining about how I speed up too much. Please forgive the tonal quality, my lips are always chapped beyond repair and my lungs hate me.
